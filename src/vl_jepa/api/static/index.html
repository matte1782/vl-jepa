Features

Everything you need to master lectures

Powerful AI tools designed to help students and educators extract maximum value from lecture content.

Visual Search

Search through visual content using DINOv2 embeddings. Find slides, diagrams, and visual concepts instantly.

  • Frame-by-frame analysis
  • Semantic visual matching
  • Scene change detection

Transcript Analysis

Automatic speech-to-text with Whisper AI. Full transcripts with timestamps and speaker detection.

  • Whisper-powered transcription
  • Timestamp synchronization
  • Multi-language support

Event Detection

Automatically detect key moments like slide changes, topic transitions, and important segments.

  • Slide transition detection
  • Topic boundary identification
  • Key moment highlighting

Flexible Export

Export your analysis in multiple formats. Create study notes, subtitles, or integrate with other tools.

  • Markdown summaries
  • JSON structured data
  • SRT subtitle files
Process

How it works

Three simple steps to transform your lectures into searchable knowledge

1

Upload Video

Drag and drop your lecture video or click to browse. We support MP4, WebM, AVI, MOV, and MKV formats up to 500MB.

2

AI Processing

Our pipeline extracts audio, transcribes speech, samples frames, generates embeddings, and detects events automatically.

3

Search & Export

Search through transcripts and visual content with natural language queries. Export results in your preferred format.
